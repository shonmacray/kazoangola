About Us

Kazo Angola Sacco was established in 2001 with the aim of improving the social-economic status of the stakeholders within its cafohment area. It registered with UCSCU in 2004 with certificate - No. 7152.

It was in the late 90's, when gentlemen Mr. John Nekedde, Charles Kiwuuwa and Nakityo Immaculate who were in charge of KAZO-ANGOLA parish, all employees of plan international-Kampala, situated by then at Mbogo Kewempe division. With the help of community resource persons, Mr. Balamaga Samuel Mujunga & Baagala Joyce for lugoba Village, Nakubulwa and Kakoza for Kazo-Angola central, Nakivumbi Betty and another for Corner zone mobilized residents to from foster family groups.

Our Mission

To be reliable members owned financial insitution that provide diversited servicies to enhance community transformation

Objectives

The major objective is to transform the lives of the people of kawempe Division and the surrounding community of wakiso through provision of start income generating projects
To harness the youths and elderly entrepreneurship capabilities, we have done this through provision of start up loans ranging from 100,000/= to 1,000,000/= and provision of business counsel which have improved thier business management skills.
Provision of apprenticeship to our members and community at large through our members founded projects
